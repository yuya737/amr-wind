Detailed Description

Collection of data sampling objects

A concrete impelementation of the post-processing interface that deals with data probes.

Multiple instances of sampling entities (e.g., point cloud or probes, line, or plane samplers) can be registered. Internally, the data probles are represented as amr_wind::sampling::SamplingContainer for tracking their position within the CFD mesh and updating the fields of interest.

This class manages the interpolation and the field management. The actual definition of probes are managed by subclasses of SamplerBase.
